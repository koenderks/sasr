3.4 Step 3: Execution

The next step in the audit sampling workflow is to test the sample. This involves reviewing the transactions or records that were selected for the sample and testing them to determine if they are accurate. This may involve reviewing documentation, such as invoices or receipts, to verify that the transactions or records are accurate.
